Delete your TorqueDen account

Applies to the TorqueDen app (developer: TorqueDen)

You can permanently delete your TorqueDen account and its data at any time, directly from the app. This page explains how, and exactly what happens to your data when you do.

How to delete your account

  1. Open TorqueDen and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Scroll to the Danger zone section and tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Deletion happens immediately and cannot be undone. If you can no longer sign in to request deletion this way, email torquedenapp@gmail.com from the address on your account and we'll delete it for you.

What gets deleted

DataWhat happens
Account & login (email, password hash)Deleted immediately
Profile (display name, username, bio, avatar)Deleted immediately
Cars, build logs, specsDeleted immediately
Uploaded photos and videos (car photos, post media, club images)Deleted immediately (best-effort storage cleanup runs as part of account deletion, so it never blocks the deletion itself)
Posts, comments, club memberships, follows, likesDeleted immediately (cascades automatically from your account)
Notification & location preferencesDeleted immediately
BackupsMay persist for a short period before being overwritten as part of routine backup rotation

Content you posted publicly may already have been seen, quoted, or screenshotted by other users before deletion — we can't remove copies made outside the app.

Questions

For anything about account deletion or your data, contact torquedenapp@gmail.com. See our full Privacy Policy for more detail on what we collect and your rights.
