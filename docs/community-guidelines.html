Community Guidelines

Effective 5 July 2026

Keep it about the builds

TorqueDen is for car people to share builds, ask questions, and find their crew. Keep posts and clubs relevant and constructive.

Be respectful

Treat others how you’d want to be treated. No harassment, bullying, hate speech, threats, or targeting people over race, religion, gender, sexuality, disability, or nationality.

Not allowed

  • Illegal content or promoting dangerous or illegal activity.
  • Sexually explicit or graphic violent content.
  • Spam, scams, or misleading content.
  • Sharing others’ private information without consent.
  • Impersonation, or infringing someone else’s copyright or trademarks.

Reporting & consequences

If you see something that breaks these guidelines, report it (long-press or use the report option) or block the user. We review reports and act — usually within 24 hours — by removing content and, where needed, removing the user. Serious or repeated breaches lead to account termination.

Questions

Not sure if something’s allowed? Ask us at torquedenapp@gmail.com.
